About Me

Hi, I'm Sean McWilliams, a UX Designer and Creative based in Raleigh, NC.

What I Do

I specialize in creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. My work spans UX research, product strategy, information architecture, and interaction design. I believe great design happens when we deeply understand user needs and translate them into elegant solutions.

Background

With a background in both design and technology, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving. I've worked with startups, agencies, and established companies to ship products that users love.

Approach

I'm driven by curiosity and systematic thinking. Whether it's conducting user research, mapping complex systems, or crafting pixel-perfect interfaces, I focus on understanding the "why" behind design decisions.

Beyond Design

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring BMX history, experimenting with creative coding, or diving into emerging tech trends.
